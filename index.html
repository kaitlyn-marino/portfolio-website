About Me

Welcome! I am so glad you are here with me today. I am thrilled to have the opportunity to share my love of web development with you.

As a front end developer, I pride myself on writing elegant, clean code with comments to ensure optimal efficiency. I am passionate about stylizing front end components with CSS and implementing fluid, responsive design.

My specialty lies in converting PSD files to live, functional websites meeting all requirements outlined in the mock-ups. My previous professional experience in graphic design has guided me into web design and UX design.

My personal goal for every client is to deliver an exceptional web experience for each of their customers while implementing best practices and the latest standards and trends.

I am always intrigued by a new challenge and enjoy seeing my skillset continually grow. Take a look around and get in touch!
